Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 2026  ·  We are committed to transparency regarding data collected on our website.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by a web browser when you visit a website. They allow websites to recognize your device, remember your preferences, and improve your overall browsing experience. Cookies may be session-based (deleted when you close your browser) or persistent (stored for a defined period).

2. How We Use Cookies

Enissen Manufacturing uses cookies strictly for functional and analytical purposes. We do not use cookies to serve personalized advertisements or share data with third parties for commercial gain.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Essential Cookies Enable core website functionality such as page navigation and form submissions. The website cannot function properly without these. Session
Preference Cookies Remember user preferences (e.g., language or display settings) to provide a consistent, personalized browsing experience. Up to 12 months
Analytics Cookies Collect anonymized usage data (e.g., pages visited, session duration) to help us understand how our website is used and improve its performance. Up to 24 months

4. Third-Party Cookies

Our website may load resources from trusted third-party services (such as Google Fonts or analytics providers). These services may set their own cookies in accordance with their respective privacy policies. Enissen Manufacturing does not control third-party cookies and recommends that users review the privacy policies of any third-party services they encounter.

5. Managing Cookies

You have full control over the cookies stored on your device. You may:

  • Configure your browser to block all cookies or notify you when a cookie is being set.
  • Delete existing cookies at any time through your browser settings.
  • Use your browser in private/incognito mode to prevent persistent cookies from being stored.

Please note that disabling essential cookies may affect the functionality of certain features on our website. The following links provide cookie management instructions for the most common browsers:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

6. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this Cookie Policy at any time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with a revised effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we use cookies.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about our use of cookies, please reach out through our Contact page. We are committed to providing clear and transparent information regarding our data practices.
