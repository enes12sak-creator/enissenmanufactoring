Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026  ·  Your privacy is of the utmost importance to us.

1. Introduction

Enissen Manufacturing ("Enissen," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy and personal data of its clients, partners, and website visitors. This Privacy Policy outlines what data we collect, how we use it, and the rights available to you in connection with your personal information.

2. Data We Collect

We collect only the minimum data necessary to provide our services effectively. This may include:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, and any other information voluntarily provided through our contact form.
  • Technical Documents: Engineering drawings, CAD files, material specifications, and related documentation submitted for quotation or production purposes.
  • Communication Data: Email correspondence and any other written communications between you and Enissen Manufacturing.
  • Usage Data: Anonymized and aggregated data on how users interact with our website (e.g., pages visited, session duration).

3. How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • To respond to inquiries and provide accurate price quotations.
  • To fulfill agreed production and delivery services.
  • To issue and manage invoices and contractual documentation.
  • To improve our website and service offerings based on aggregated usage insights.
  • To comply with legal obligations under applicable Turkish and international law.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing or commercial purposes.

4. Data Security

Enissen Manufacturing employs industry-standard technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. All technical documents and client communications are stored securely and access is strictly limited to authorized personnel.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal data and technical documentation for as long as necessary to fulfill our service obligations and comply with legal requirements. Once data is no longer required, it is securely deleted or anonymized in accordance with applicable regulations.

6. Your Rights

Subject to applicable law, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention requirements.
  • Object to the processing of your data in certain circumstances.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us through our Contact page.

7. Third-Party Services

Our website may use third-party services (such as database providers or analytics tools) strictly for operational purposes. All such providers are selected based on their compliance with applicable data protection standards and are bound by confidentiality agreements.

8. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any material changes will be reflected on this page with a revised effective date. Continued use of our services following such changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
